Happy Birthday My Darling SUNMBO,
Today has always being a special day right from when we were kids,
OMOBONIKE, omo Papi Craig.
Happy 49th Birthday! wow its being 49 years of being your sister, It feels just like yesterday we were kids, playing together in the yard of Dad's house, going to school together while i hold your hands as we journey on, whispering secrets and gists late into the night as we speak about our future.
As the years fly by, one thing remians same, unchanged, undiminished and has only grown stronger: my love, admiration and appreciation for you.

You are not just my sister, you're my confidante, keeper of so many cherished memories. From dreams, aspirations to navigating life in its full glory, you have been my constant.

Through every twists and turns,
every challenges and triumph,
every tear shed and echoes of joyful laughter, you have been there. Moments of pure joy we shared, the kind that only sisters can understand. Those memories are forever etched into my heart. My rock and biggest cheerleader.

Today isn't to talk about past memories but to celebrate the icon you are,
You've embodied the true meaning of Sisterhood and most importantly MOTHERHOOD, loyalty, kindness and care. Your strength have inspired me on days when i felt weak, in countless ways, you've often offered a different view, offering wisdom when needed.
Your presence in my life has been and will always be an immeasurable gift, one that I cherish more than wors or actions would ever say.

As you celebrate this milestone,
I want you to always know how deeply you are loved and cherished. You deserve all the Happiness, Joy, and Peace the world has to offer.

On this day and for ever, May your days be filled with joy, laughter, happiness, peace, good health, prosperity, and wealth always; May this year bring you closer to all your dreams, all the happiness, that you deserve.
I pray that God continually sends his Angels to keep watch over you in your going out and coming in, whatever you lay your hands will always be prosperous, we will never weep over you, you shall know no sorrow, in your finances; Gods blessings is over it. I pray God makes everything concerning your life easy, beautiful, fruitful, prosperous and, joyful. AMEN

Cheers to your new age, a new phase, a bright beginning of Good News.
Thank you, My Beautiful Sister, for being YOU. Thank you for every laugh, every hugs, every shared memories and moments.
Thank you for giving me Handsome ADEOGO and Beautiful INIOGO

May God grant you long life in good health and wealth to eat the fruits of your labour.
May your days be filled with unending joy and happiness in all ways to the Gory of Almighty God, IJMNA.

I love you Sister, IGBA ODUN, ODUN KAN

With love,
